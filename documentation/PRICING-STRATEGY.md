**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** December 14, 2025  
**Status:** Draft for Review

## Executive Summary

ClubOps pricing is designed around a **modular, scalable model** that grows with the customer. We offer a core SaaS platform with optional hardware add-ons, creating multiple revenue streams and high customer lifetime value.

**Revenue Streams:**
1. SaaS Subscriptions (recurring monthly/annual)
2. Transaction Fees (POS processing)
3. Hardware Lease/Purchase
4. Implementation & Setup Fees
5. Premium Support Tiers

| Component | Monthly | One-Time Setup |
|-----------|---------|----------------|
| Front Door POS | $99/mo | $299 |
| VIP POS Terminal | $79/mo/terminal | $199/terminal |

### Transaction Processing

| Transaction Type | Rate |
|------------------|------|
| Credit/Debit | 2.5% + $0.25 |
| ACH/Bank Transfer | 1.0% + $0.50 |
| Cash Recording | Free |

**Revenue Potential (Example Club):**
- 500 transactions/night × $0.25 = $125/night
- 500 × $50 avg × 2.5% = $625/night
- **Total:** ~$750/night = **$22,500/month** in processing fees per location

## Part 9: Pricing Recommendations

### Phase 1: Launch (Current)
1. **Focus on SaaS adoption** — Get clubs on the platform
2. **Freemium hook** — Starter tier drives trials
3. **Professional tier sweet spot** — $99/mo is accessible
4. **Lock in annual contracts** — 20% discount drives commitment

### Phase 2: POS Rollout (Q1 2025)
1. **Bundle incentives** — Discount POS for existing SaaS customers
2. **Transaction fee focus** — High-volume revenue stream
3. **Pilot program** — 10 beta clubs for POS validation

### Phase 3: Hardware Expansion (Q2-Q3 2025)
1. **Lease-first model** — Lower barrier to adoption
2. **Installation partnerships** — Local AV/security companies
3. **Financing options** — For hardware purchases

### Phase 4: Scale (Q4 2025+)
1. **Multi-location pricing** — Volume discounts for chains
2. **API monetization** — Developer ecosystem
3. **White-label licensing** — Franchise/partner revenue
