# Part 2: Core Operations

## Chapter 3: Onboarding New Dancers

Before a dancer can work at your club, they need to complete the onboarding process. ClubOps makes this simple and paperless.

### Sending an Application Link

1. Go to **Dancers** in the left menu
2. Click the **+ Add New Dancer** button (top right)
3. Select **Send Application Link**
4. Enter the dancer's email address
5. Click **Send Invitation**

The dancer receives an email with a secure link to complete their application.

### What the Dancer Completes

When a dancer clicks their unique link, they'll:
1. Fill out their personal information
2. Upload a photo of their license/ID
3. Enter their license expiration date
4. Read and digitally sign the club contract
5. Submit their application

**Important:** The dancer does NOT need to create an accountâ€”their link is unique and secure.

### Reviewing Applications

When applications come in:

1. Go to **Dancers** â†’ **Pending Applications**
2. Click on a dancer's name to review their submission
3. Verify their license information matches their uploaded ID
4. Review the signed contract

To approve:
- Click the green **Approve** button
- The dancer is now in your system and ready to check in

To deny:
- Click the red **Deny** button
- Optionally add a reason (the dancer will see this)

## Chapter 5: DJ Queue Management

The DJ interface is the heart of ClubOpsâ€”designed for fast, fluid stage management.

### Accessing the DJ Interface

1. Go to **DJ Queue** in the left menu
2. You'll see three main areas:
   - **Available Dancers** (left panel) â€” Checked-in dancers waiting for rotation
   - **Stage Queues** (center) â€” Drag-and-drop line-ups for each stage
   - **Now Performing** (right panel) â€” Current dancer on each stage with timer

### Adding Dancers to the Queue

**Method 1: Drag and Drop**
1. Find the dancer in "Available Dancers"
2. Click and hold their name card
3. Drag to the desired position in a stage queue
4. Release to drop them in place

**Method 2: Quick Add**
1. Click the **+** button on any stage queue
2. Select a dancer from the dropdown
3. They're added to the end of that queue

### Rearranging the Queue

To change the order:
1. Click and hold any dancer in a queue
2. Drag them up or down to the new position
3. Release to confirm

The queue automatically reorders and updates on all screens in real-time.

### Starting a Performance

When it's time for a dancer to take the stage:

1. The dancer at the top of the queue is highlighted
2. Click **Send to Stage** (or drag them to the "Now Performing" area)
3. The timer automatically starts
4. The next dancer moves to the top of the queue

### Managing Multiple Stages

If your club has multiple stages:

1. Each stage has its own queue column
2. Drag dancers between stage queues as needed
3. Each stage's "Now Performing" timer runs independently

## Chapter 12: Offline Mode

ClubOps continues working even if your internet goes down.

### How Offline Mode Works

When internet connection is lost:
- A yellow **"Offline Mode"** banner appears at the top
- You can still:
  - Check dancers in and out
  - Manage the DJ queue
  - Track VIP sessions
  - View dancer profiles

### What Happens When You Reconnect

When internet returns:
1. The yellow banner disappears
2. All offline transactions automatically sync to the cloud
3. A brief **"Syncing..."** message appears
4. Everything is up to date within seconds

**Important:** No data is lost during offline periods. Everything syncs when you're back online.

# Part 6: Troubleshooting

## Chapter 13: Common Issues & Solutions

### "I can't log in"

**Try these steps:**
1. Check that you're using the correct email address
2. Click "Forgot Password" to reset it
3. Check your spam folder for the reset email
4. Make sure CAPS LOCK is off
5. Try a different browser

Still stuck? Contact your club administrator.

### "A dancer can't check in"

**Check these things:**
1. Is their license expired? (Look for red alert)
2. Are they in the system? (Search in Dancers list)
3. Was their application approved?

### "The queue isn't updating on all screens"

**This usually means a connection issue:**
1. Check the internet connection
2. Refresh the page (press F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. If offline, changes will sync when reconnected

### "I can't hear the music"

**Check these settings:**
1. Is the volume turned up in ClubOps?
2. Is your device volume turned up?
3. Is the correct audio output selected on your device?
4. Try clicking the play button again

### "Reports show wrong numbers"

**Verify the date range:**
1. Check the start and end dates of your report
2. Make sure you've selected the correct report type
3. If checking today's numbers, make sure all sessions are ended properly

### "I uploaded a song but can't find it"

**Wait for processing:**
1. Uploads take a moment to process
2. Check the upload progress indicator
3. Look in the "Recently Added" section
4. Try refreshing the music library

# Part 7: Best Practices

## Chapter 14: Tips for Success

### For Owners & Managers

**Check compliance weekly:**
- Every Monday, review the License Status report
- Contact dancers with licenses expiring in the next 30 days
- Don't wait until licenses expire

**Review finances daily:**
- Check the dashboard at end of night
- Follow up on deferred payments over 3 days old
- Export weekly reports for your records

**Keep staff trained:**
- Have new staff shadow experienced team members
- Review this manual with them
- Make sure everyone knows their access level

### For Door Staff

**Verify every check-in:**
- Always confirm the license status light is green or yellow
- If yellow, verbally remind the dancer to renew soon
- Never override a red/expired license block

**Handle bar fees consistently:**
- Collect upfront when possible
- For deferred payments, remind dancers before checkout
- Log everythingâ€”don't rely on memory

### For DJs

**Keep the queue organized:**
- Build the queue at least 3-4 dancers deep
- Communicate with dancers about their expected times
- Use the "Notify Next" feature to give dancers a heads-up

**Leverage playlists:**
- Set up playlists for regular dancers
- It speeds up your workflow
- Dancers appreciate that you remember their songs

**Monitor VIP timers:**
- Keep an eye on the sidebar timers
- Give dancers and customers 5-minute warnings
- End sessions promptly to maintain accurate records
