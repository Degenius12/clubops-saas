**Goal**: 5-10 paying customers in 3-6 months
**Budget**: $0-$100/month
**Strategy**: High-effort, low-cost tactics

- Leverage existing beta customers
- Build lead list (500 clubs)
- Set up email infrastructure

### Phase 2: Content Creation (Weeks 3-4)
- Create marketing assets
- Write LinkedIn articles
- Record demo video

### Phase 3: Outreach (Weeks 5-10)
- Warm referral outreach
- Cold email campaigns
- LinkedIn networking

### Phase 4: Conversion (Weeks 11-12)
- Optimize demo process
- Free trial onboarding

### Phase 5: Scale (Months 4-6)
- Expand to Tier 2/3 clubs
- Content marketing momentum
- Prepare for ED EXPO 2026

## 💡 Tips for Success

1. **Leverage Beta Customers First**
   - They're your best source of testimonials and referrals
   - Get case studies with real metrics
   - Ask for 2-3 warm introductions each

2. **Quality Over Quantity**
   - 10 personalized emails > 100 generic blasts
   - Research each club before reaching out
   - Mention specific observations in emails

3. **Focus on Your Strengths**
   - You're strong at demos/sales calls
   - Your goal is to GET to the call
   - Let your sales skills close the deal

4. **Track Everything**
   - Every email sent, opened, replied
   - Every demo booked, completed, converted
   - Learn from what works, kill what doesn't

5. **Be Patient with Content**
   - LinkedIn articles take 3-6 months to compound
   - Start now, reap benefits later
   - Consistency beats perfection

## 🚨 Red Flags

**After 2 weeks**:
- No email replies → Check deliverability
- Opens but no replies → Improve copy
- No LinkedIn engagement → Change topics

**After 1 month**:
- No demos → Lower barrier to entry
- Demos but no trials → Better demo
- Trials but no conversions → Onboarding issue

**After 3 months**:
- No customers → Reassess product-market fit
