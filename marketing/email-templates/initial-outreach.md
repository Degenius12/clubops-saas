# Initial Outreach Email Template

## Subject Line Options (A/B Test These):
1. Quick question about [Club Name]
2. Still managing dancers in Excel, [FirstName]?
3. [Club Name] - VIP revenue tracking question
4. Saw your club's 4.5★ rating – impressive

## Email Body (Keep under 100 words):

```
Hi [FirstName],

Noticed [Club Name] has [specific observation - e.g., "3 VIP suites" or "great reviews on Yelp" or "recently renovated"].

Most clubs your size lose 15-20% of VIP revenue to tracking errors and fraud. We built ClubFlow to fix that - it's used by [reference club] and saves them ~$2K/month on average.

Worth a 10-min conversation?

Best,
[Your Name]
Founder, ClubFlow
[Phone Number]
clubflowapp.com
```

## Personalization Checklist:
- [ ] Replace [FirstName] with actual first name
- [ ] Replace [Club Name] with actual club name
- [ ] Add specific observation (check Yelp/Google reviews)
- [ ] Use local reference club if possible
- [ ] Include YOUR phone number

## Sending Best Practices:
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm recipient's timezone
- Plain text only (no HTML)
- Wait 3 days before follow-up
- Track opens/replies in outreach-tracker.xlsx

## Expected Metrics:
- Open rate: 35-45%
- Reply rate: 8-12%
- Demo booking: 5-8%
