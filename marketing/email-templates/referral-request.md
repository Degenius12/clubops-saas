# Referral Request Email

## When to Send:
- After customer has been using ClubFlow for 30+ days
- After they've had a "win" (caught fraud, saved time, etc.)
- After positive feedback or testimonial

## Subject Line:
Quick favor?

## Email Body:

```
Hey [FirstName],

Glad ClubFlow is working well for [Club Name]!

Quick ask: Do you know 2-3 other club owners/GMs who might benefit from this?

We're offering:
- $500 account credit for each referral that becomes a customer
- FREE lifetime account if you refer 3+ clubs

Just introduce us via email and I'll take it from there.

Appreciate you!

[Your Name]

P.S. Here's a 1-pager you can forward if helpful: [link to sales sheet]
```

## Incentive Structure:
- 1 referral = $500 credit
- 2 referrals = $1,000 credit
- 3+ referrals = Free lifetime account (saves $1,188/year)

## Follow-Up:
- If they refer someone, send thank you + update on progress
- Pay out credit immediately when referral converts
- Send reminder every 3-6 months
