# Break-Up Email (Day 10)

## Subject Line:
Closing the loop

## Email Body:

```
[FirstName], I'll stop bothering you :)

But if you ever want to see how clubs like [Local Competitor Name] are preventing fraud and saving time with ClubFlow, just hit reply.

No hard feelings either way.

[Your Name]
Founder, ClubFlow
```

## Why This Works:
- Pattern interrupt (reverse psychology)
- Mention local competitor (creates FOMO)
- Gives them easy out (reduces pressure)
- Often gets highest reply rate of entire sequence

## When to Send:
- Day 10 after initial email
- FINAL email in sequence
- Don't follow up after this unless they reply

## Expected Results:
- 15-20% reply rate (highest of any email)
- Many will say "not now but keep in touch"
- Some will immediately book a demo
