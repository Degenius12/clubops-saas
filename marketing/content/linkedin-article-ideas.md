## Distribution Strategy

### Posting Schedule:
- **Frequency**: 1 article per week
- **Best Days**: Tuesday or Wednesday
- **Best Time**: 8-10am ET (when club owners check LinkedIn)

### Promotion Tactics:
1. **LinkedIn Post**: Share with personal commentary
2. **Relevant Groups**: Post in "Nightclub Owners," "Hospitality Management"
3. **Tag Industry Leaders**: Mention ACE National, ED Publications
4. **Engage**: Reply to every comment within 24 hours
5. **Cross-Post**: Republish on Medium after 1 week

### Engagement Goals:
- 500-1,000 views per article
- 10-20 reactions
- 5-10 comments
- 2-5 profile visits
- 1-2 DM conversations

### Content Upgrades:
- Embed downloadable checklists
- Link to case studies
- Include screenshots of ClubFlow
- Add customer quotes

### SEO Optimization:
- Use target keywords in headline
- Include keywords in first paragraph
- Add 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Link to clubflowapp.com
