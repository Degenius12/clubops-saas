## Quick Decision Matrix

| Situation | Tier | Reason |
|-----------|------|--------|
| < 15 entertainers | Tier 1 | Small enough to enter manually |
| Has spreadsheet already | Tier 1 | Just needs to convert to CSV |
| 15-50 entertainers, no spreadsheet | Tier 2 | You do import, they provide data |
| 50+ entertainers | Tier 2 or 3 | Tier 2 if tech-savvy, Tier 3 if not |
| Customer #1-10 (any size) | Tier 3 | Worth your time for learning |
| Customer is hesitant/skeptical | Tier 3 | Build trust with hands-on help |
| Customer says "too busy" | Tier 3 | Remove all friction |
| Enterprise/chain (multi-location) | Tier 3 | High LTV, worth the investment |

---

## Red Flags → Always Upgrade to Tier 3

Watch for these signals during demo:
- "I don't have time for this"
- "I'm not good with computers"
- "Can you just do it for me?"
- "This seems complicated"
- Trial signup but no activity for 24 hours

**Action**: Proactively call and offer white-glove setup

## Scripts for Each Tier

### Offering Tier 1:
```
"Great! I'll send you a simple template. Just fill in stage names
and phone numbers - takes about 15 minutes. Upload it to ClubFlow
and I'll call you tomorrow to make sure everything looks good."
```

### Offering Tier 2:
```
"Here's what I'll do - fill out this quick form with your dancers'
names and numbers. I'll import everything for you and we'll review
it together on a call. Sound good?"
```

### Offering Tier 3:
```
"Let's make this super easy. We can hop on a 30-minute call, you
read me the names and numbers, I'll type them in while showing you
how everything works. When works for you?"
```

## Time Investment Guide

| Your Time | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|-----------|--------|--------|--------|
| Preparation | 5 min (send template) | 5 min (send form) | 5 min (prep for call) |
| Data Entry | 0 min (they do it) | 30 min (you do it) | 30 min (live with customer) |
| Review Call | 15 min | 30 min | Included in data entry |
| **TOTAL** | **20 min** | **65 min** | **35 min** |

**Conversion Rate**:
- Tier 1: 20-30% (some abandon)
- Tier 2: 40-50% (moderate friction)
- Tier 3: 70-80% (highest touch)

**ROI**:
- Tier 1: Lowest time, lowest conversion
- Tier 2: Medium time, medium conversion
- Tier 3: Moderate time, highest conversion ← **BEST for first 20 customers**

## When Customer Asks "How long will this take?"

### Tier 1 Response:
"About 15-20 minutes to fill out the template, then 1 minute to upload.
I'll verify everything the next day to make sure it's perfect."

### Tier 2 Response:
"You: 5-10 minutes to fill the form. Me: I handle the import.
Then we review together in 30 minutes. Total: Under an hour,
and most of that is me working."

### Tier 3 Response:
"30 minutes on a call with me. You read names and numbers, I type
everything in while showing you the system. Done in one session."

## Common Objections & Tier Upgrades

### "I'm too busy to do data entry"
→ Upgrade to Tier 3
```
"Totally understand. Let me handle it for you. I'll call you
during your slow time, you just read me the names, I'll take
care of the rest. When's good? 2-5pm today?"
```

### "I don't have a spreadsheet"
→ Tier 2 or 3
```
"No problem! Two options:
1. Fill out this 5-minute form, I'll import everything (Tier 2)
2. Jump on a call, you dictate, I type (Tier 3)

Which sounds easier?"
```

### "This seems complicated"
→ Tier 3
```
"I hear you. That's why I do the first setup for you.
Zero work on your end - I'll build your roster while walking
you through how it works. Sound better?"
```

### "I have 200+ entertainers"
→ Tier 2 + phased approach
```
"Let's start with your top 30-40 active ones. Get you
running this week, then we'll add the rest as they work
shifts. How's that sound?"
```

## Scaling Rules

### Customers 1-10:
**Default**: Tier 3 (white-glove everything)
**Why**: Learning, building relationships, case studies

### Customers 11-20:
**Default**: Tier 2 (hybrid)
**Upgrade to Tier 3**: If high-value or struggling

### Customers 21-50:
**Default**: Tier 1 (self-service)
**Upgrade to Tier 2/3**: If they request help or stall

### Customers 51+:
**Default**: Tier 1 (self-service)
**Automation**: Hire VA to handle Tier 2 imports
**You only do**: High-value Tier 3 (enterprises, multi-location)

## Success Checklist

After onboarding (any tier), verify:
- [ ] At least 10 entertainers imported
- [ ] Customer has logged in
- [ ] Customer completed 1 check-in (demo or real)
- [ ] Follow-up call scheduled (Day 3)
- [ ] Customer knows how to contact you

**If ANY checkbox is unchecked → high churn risk → call immediately**

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Always offer next level down first**
   - Start with Tier 1, upgrade if needed
   - Customers appreciate the choice

2. **Watch for stalls at 48 hours**
   - No activity = call and offer Tier 3
   - "Hey, want me to just do this for you?"

3. **Batch Tier 2 imports**
   - Do 5 customers at once (2.5 hours)
   - More efficient than one-at-a-time

4. **Record Tier 3 calls**
   - Ask permission first
   - Use for training future VAs
   - Extract testimonials

5. **Time-box each tier**
   - Tier 1: Max 20 min
   - Tier 2: Max 60 min
   - Tier 3: Max 90 min
   - If exceeding, that's a data quality issue (fix process)
