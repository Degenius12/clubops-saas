# ClubFlow Demo Script (30 Minutes)

## Pre-Demo Checklist:
- [ ] Confirm they received calendar invite with Zoom link
- [ ] Test screen share and audio
- [ ] Have demo environment loaded with realistic data
- [ ] Know their club name, size, and key pain points
- [ ] Have follow-up email template ready

---

## Part 1: Discovery (10 minutes)

### Opening:
"Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. Before I show you anything, I want to make sure I'm showing you what's most relevant to [Club Name]. Cool if I ask a few quick questions?"

### Key Questions:
1. **Current Setup**: "How are you currently managing [dancers/VIP/scheduling]?"
2. **Pain Points**: "What's your biggest operational headache right now?"
3. **Fraud/Revenue**: "Ever had issues with VIP tracking or tip-out discrepancies?"
4. **Size**: "How many dancers do you typically have on a Friday/Saturday night?"
5. **Decision**: "If this solves [their pain point], what's your process for moving forward?"

### Listen For:
- Specific problems (fraud, time waste, compliance)
- Budget constraints
- Decision-making authority
- Timeline for implementation
- Current frustrations with existing tools

---

## Part 2: Tailored Demo (15 minutes)

### Option A: If Fraud/Revenue is Priority

**Start Here: Security Dashboard**
1. Show real-time fraud alerts
2. Explain triple-verification system
3. Demo: "See this alert? This caught a $500 discrepancy"
4. Show statistical anomaly detection with confidence scores
5. **Value statement**: "Clubs like [Reference Club] save $2K/month with this"

**Then Show: VIP Booth Tracking**
1. Real-time spending vs minimum
2. Automatic alerts at 80% capacity
3. Time-based or song-based billing
4. **Value statement**: "Prevents short-pays on VIP minimums"

### Option B: If Efficiency/Time is Priority

**Start Here: Entertainer Management**
1. Digital check-in/check-out (vs paper logbook)
2. Automated house fee calculation
3. Photo management, license tracking
4. **Value statement**: "Saves 15-20 hours/week on admin"

**Then Show: Shift Scheduling**
1. Weekly calendar view
2. Shift swap requests
3. Automated notifications
4. **Value statement**: "No more group text chaos"

### Option C: If Compliance is Priority

**Start Here: Compliance Dashboard**
1. State-specific workflows
2. License expiry alerts (30/60/90 days)
3. Digital contract signing
4. 2257 compliance automation
5. **Value statement**: "Never miss a renewal, avoid $10K+ fines"

### Universal Features to Always Show:
1. **DJ Queue** (crowd favorite - drag-and-drop demo)
2. **Revenue Dashboard** (real-time updates)
3. **Mobile app** (check in from anywhere)

---

## Part 3: Close & Next Steps (5 minutes)

### Trial Offer:
"What I'd like to do is get you set up with a 14-day trial so you can see this in action with your own dancers. I'll personally help you import your first 10-20 dancers and get everything configured for [Club Name]."

### Handle Objections:

**"I need to think about it"**
→ "Totally understand. What specific concerns do you have?"
→ Address concerns directly
→ "How about we start the trial while you're thinking? No credit card needed."

**"What's the cost?"**
→ "For a club your size, it's $99/month. But ROI is usually 10-20x that in fraud prevention alone."
→ "We also offer 40% off first 3 months if you sign up during trial."

**"I need to talk to my partner/owner"**
→ "Makes sense. Want to bring them on a quick call? I can do 15 min tomorrow."
→ "Or I can send you a 1-pager you can share with them."

**"We're using [Competitor/Excel]"**
→ "What do you like about it?"
→ "What would you change if you could?"
→ Focus on ClubFlow's strengths in those areas

### Booking Next Step:
"Let's schedule a 15-min check-in call for [3 days from now] to make sure you're getting value from the trial. Does [day/time] work?"

### Send Follow-Up Email (Within 5 Minutes):
```
[Name], great talking to you!

Here's your trial login: [link]
And that case study I mentioned: [link]

I'll reach out on [day] to check in, but feel free to text/call if you need anything: [phone]

Looking forward to working together!

[Your Name]
```

---

## Demo Don'ts:
- ❌ Don't show every feature (overwhelming)
- ❌ Don't talk for more than 2-3 min without asking a question
- ❌ Don't badmouth competitors
- ❌ Don't say "Does that make sense?" (condescending)
- ❌ Don't end without a clear next step

## Demo Do's:
- ✅ Start with their biggest pain point
- ✅ Use their club name throughout
- ✅ Tell stories ("Here's what happened at [Club]...")
- ✅ Ask "How would this work for you?"
- ✅ Focus on ROI and time savings
- ✅ Get commitment to trial before ending call

---

## Post-Demo Follow-Up Sequence:
1. **Day 0**: Send trial login + case study
2. **Day 1**: "Did you get a chance to log in?"
3. **Day 3**: Check-in call (scheduled during demo)
4. **Day 7**: Share another case study
5. **Day 10**: "Trial expires in 4 days - let's lock in your discount"

---

## Demo Customization Notes:
- **Multi-location owners**: Show club switcher, aggregate analytics
- **Large clubs (30+ dancers)**: Emphasize scalability, automation
- **Small clubs (10-15 dancers)**: Show how quick/easy it is
- **Tech-savvy**: Show API access, integrations
- **Tech-skeptical**: Focus on simplicity, support
